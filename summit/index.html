Annual Summit

The Open Company Summit takes place in March, and represents the best opportunity for the members of the Open Company Initiative to learn from one another and further the vision of the Initiative.

2014

The 2014 Summit was held on March 22 in San Francisco. A poorly-recorded video of the event was archived for posterity.

2013

The predecessor to the Open Company Summit was an “Open Business Open House,” hosted by Balanced Payments in San Francisco on March 20. It featured a panel discussion between the CEOs of Balanced, Gittip, Trovebox, and Watsi, moderated by the program manager of Mozilla WebFWD.
