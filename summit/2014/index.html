Summit 2014

Agenda: Crepes and warmup for 45 minutes, followed by 90 minutes of roundtable conversation about our companies and the direction of the Open Company Initiative.

Where: Balanced Payments, 965 Mission Street, Suite 425

Discussion and planning happened via GitHub.
